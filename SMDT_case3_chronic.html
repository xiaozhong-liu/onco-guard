Temporal Pattern Detection

Cross-Domain Correlation
Psychiatric records: PHQ-9 scores submitted to Dr. Navarro are consistently higher in luteal phase (+4.2 pts avg). Insulin pump logs: manual basal rate adjustments increase 2.3× in same window — autonomic dysregulation affecting insulin sensitivity.
⚠ Pregnancy Risk Inference
If this pattern persists in early pregnancy: luteal-phase-like hormonal profile is sustained for 12+ weeks. Autonomic instability + disrupted sleep + depression exacerbation during first trimester = compounding risk. Must be resolved before conception.
Pharmacological Hypothesis
Duloxetine (SNRI) inhibits norepinephrine reuptake — can amplify autonomic stress responses. Luteal-phase estrogen withdrawal may potentiate this effect. Temporal dosing adjustment is a testable intervention.
