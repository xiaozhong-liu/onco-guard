Aim 1 · Contribution
Self-Reflective On-Device Memory
First on-device SLM combining longitudinal personalized fine-tuning with conformal prediction UQ — transforms local AI from a static record into an active, self-reflective diagnostic asset that knows the limits of its own knowledge.
Aim 2 · Contribution
Active Strategic Privacy Mediation
Shifts privacy from passive filtering to context-aware game-theoretic mediation. Disclosure adapts dynamically per turn based on clinical necessity and re-identification risk — beyond differential privacy or k-anonymity.
Aim 3 · Contribution
Diagnostic Diversity as Clinical Resource
The Diversity Prism is the first formalized mechanism treating inter-model disagreement as a measurable clinical signal — AI heterogeneity becomes a tool for bias-resistant independent second opinions.
